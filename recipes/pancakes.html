Luke's Famous Pancakes

Your ticket to breakfast happiness. Just like Grandma used to make.

  • 1.5 cups flour
  • 3.5 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • tablespoon white sugar
  • 1.25 cups milk
  • egg
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  1. Mix Dry Ingredients

    In a large bowl sift together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.

  2. Mix In Wet Ingredients

    Make a well in the center of bowl and pour in milk, egg, melted butter. Mix until smooth.

  3. Prepare Griddle

    Heat a lightly oiled griddle/frying pan over medium high heat.

  4. Cook Pancakes

    Pour or scoop batter onto hot griddle. ~1/4 cup per pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot.

Enjoy!
